Virtualisation Reading Assignment (How Did We Get Here)

Introduction

As you depart Mohawk for your career, it will be important to keep your knowledge up to date. The best way to do that is to read. Today you will have an opportunity to practice this valuable skill. You will also practice the valuable skill of summarising key points from what you have read.

You will read a series of articles, some of which are rather old, and answer questions based on their contents. The purpose of this exercise is to help you understand why the current set of virtualisation tools were created.

Grading

Provide your answers in the 'Virtualization Reading Assignment' quiz.  Try to make your answers as short as possible - short, clear writing will impress your boss and your peers.

Part C: Technical comparison

These articles ( OS vs Application Containers , VM vs Containers ) give a good technical comparison of virtual machines and containers. The author makes a distinction between O/S containers and application containers, in practice the lines between the two can be a little fuzzy so concentrate on the underlying technology as much as you can. The differences in practice will become much more clear when you use these tools to solve problems.
