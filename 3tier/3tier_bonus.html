Bonus: White Hat

The trs application we recently deployed contains at least one security vulnerability. A bounty of 5 bonus points is offered for every vulnerability reported. Only the first to report given vulnerability will earn points for that vulnerability.

Rules

  • Your exploit must be executed over the network.
  • You may attack the OS, Appache, MariaDB, PHP, or the application.
  • DOS attacks will not be rewarded.
  • Escalation of privilege attacks will be rewarded.
